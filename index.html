What We do

Education

_____

Meri Shiksha

Ngos working for education in India, Like Aiyssssa play an important role in supporting the most vulnerable children access their right to Education through innovative ideas. Education is the most important tool to lead a good life.

Health Care

_______

Ekon Care

The need of the hour is a two pronged approach- first to bring quality healthcare services to doorsteps of the needy and second to promote healthcare awareness and contemporary healthcare seeking behavior among the underprivileged.

Health Care

_______

Save Life

AIYSSSSA mission is to inspire breakthroughs in the way the world treats children and to achieve immediate and lasting change in their lives. Join us in demanding enumeration of India's street-connected children and giving them a life of dignity

Old Age

______

Elder Care

Aiyssssa is a platform that initiates a better understanding of problems of ageing from a social perspective and provides a platform for the aged as well as to those who concerned to come together to share and care.

Our approach is modeled around bringing change at all levels

With your support, we address children’s critical needs by working with parents, teachers, Anganwadi workers, communities, district and state level governments as well as the children themselves. We focus on changing behaviours and practices at the grassroots level and influencing public policy at a systemic level – thereby creating an ecosystem where children are made the nation’s priority.
